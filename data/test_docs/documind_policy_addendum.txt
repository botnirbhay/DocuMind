Section 1: Secondary Program Overview

Project Beacon is a partner onboarding initiative for enterprise channels.
The Beacon rollout began on April 18, 2026.
Beacon is sponsored by Arjun Mehta.
Implementation updates for Beacon are coordinated by Leena Joseph.

Section 2: Maintenance and Change Windows

Scheduled maintenance takes place every Saturday from 11:00 PM to 1:00 AM IST.
Emergency patches may be applied outside the maintenance window if a security issue is rated critical.
Customers are notified at least 24 hours in advance for planned maintenance work.

Section 3: Export and Retention Rules

Generated exports remain available for download for 7 days.
Audit export bundles are retained for 30 days.
Deleted exports cannot be recovered after the retention period expires.

Section 4: Regional Rollout Notes

Beacon pilot access is currently enabled for India, Singapore, and the UAE.
European rollout is planned for June 2026.
North America onboarding is dependent on regional compliance review.

Section 5: Access and Approval Rules

Only verified administrators can trigger workspace resets.
Bulk export requests require approval from the Compliance Operations team.
High-risk access changes require sign-off from Arjun Mehta and the regional compliance reviewer.

Section 6: Support Exceptions

Billing disputes for Beacon still go through Revenue Operations.
Security incidents for Beacon are routed to the Trust Engineering team.
Non-critical configuration questions are answered during standard support hours only.
